Our Story

Founded in 1992, A1 Animations began as a small collective of passionate artists who shared a love for bringing stories to life through motion. Originally operating out of a modest studio in a converted warehouse, the founders were pioneers in experimenting with both traditional hand-drawn animation and the emerging field of computer-generated imagery. Their first major project, Tales of the Midnight Sky, a short animated film, won accolades for its groundbreaking blend of 2D and 3D animation, setting the stage for the studio’s reputation for innovation.

Over the years, A1 Animations expanded its horizons, attracting top talent from around the globe. By the mid-2000s, the studio had transitioned fully into digital animation, becoming a leader in creating high-quality animations for video games, films, and television. Their collaborations with major studios and indie developers alike solidified their place as industry leaders, with blockbuster hits such as Galactic Guardians: Uprising and The Legend of Aurion to their name. Despite their success, the company remained steadfast in nurturing creativity, priding itself on being an incubator for young talent.

In 2010, the studio made a bold move to focus on education alongside production, launching its first animation course under the brand A1 Academy. Recognizing a gap in the market for comprehensive, hands-on training, the team designed courses that taught not only technical skills like rigging, modeling, and texturing but also the art of storytelling, character design, and emotional impact. These programs quickly gained accreditation and recognition, producing graduates who went on to work for major players like Pixar, Ubisoft, and Disney.

The studio’s headquarters, now a state-of-the-art facility with motion-capture labs and VR-enabled classrooms, serves as both a creative space and a training ground for the next generation of animators. A1 Animations has also embraced cutting-edge technology, integrating AI-assisted animation workflows and virtual production techniques into their curriculum and projects. The result is a unique blend of artistry and technology, where students and professionals collaborate on real-world projects, learning by doing.

One of the company’s core values is accessibility, and they offer scholarships to underrepresented communities, ensuring that anyone with a dream of animation can have a place in their program. Over the last decade, they’ve trained thousands of animators, some of whom have returned to teach or collaborate on new projects, creating a vibrant and supportive alumni network. To date, A1 Animations has expanded its educational offerings to include online courses, making their expertise available to aspiring animators worldwide.

A1 Animations remains dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what animation can achieve. Their recent project, Chronicles of the Aether, a fully interactive animated series, has been hailed as a masterpiece for seamlessly blending traditional storytelling with audience-driven choices. Meanwhile, their educational arm continues to grow, offering specialized tracks in everything from creature design to real-time animation for Unreal Engine.

What sets A1 Animations apart is its unwavering belief that animation is more than just a visual medium—it’s a way to connect, inspire, and transform. Every frame they create, whether for clients or as part of their courses, carries a piece of their rich history and dedication to the craft. From humble beginnings to global acclaim, A1 Animations has stayed true to its mission: to empower artists and tell stories that move the world.

Today, the studio stands as a beacon for aspiring animators, a hub of cutting-edge technology, and a trusted partner for some of the entertainment industry’s biggest names. Whether producing a blockbuster animated feature or mentoring a new student, A1 Animations never loses sight of its roots: a small team of dreamers determined to animate the impossible. With over three decades of excellence and innovation under its belt, the company looks forward to inspiring the next generation of storytellers, creators, and visionaries.

FAQ

What is animation?
Animation is bringing still images to life.
What is the duration of the courses?
Our courses range from 3 months to 12 months, depending on the specialization.
Do I need prior animation experience to join?
No prior experience is required. We offer beginner-friendly courses as well as advanced tracks for experienced animators.
Are the courses available online?
Yes, we offer both in-person and online courses for maximum flexibility.
What software will I learn during the course?
We cover industry-standard software such as Blender, Maya, Adobe Animate, and Unreal Engine.
Are there any scholarships available?
Yes, we offer scholarships for underrepresented communities and exceptional students.
What is the class size for the in-person courses?
Class sizes are typically between 10-20 students to ensure personalized attention.
Will I receive a certificate upon completion?
Yes, all students who complete the course will receive a certificate of completion.
Do you offer job placement assistance after graduation?
Yes, we provide job placement services, including resume workshops, interview preparation, and access to our industry network.
Can I take multiple courses at once?
Yes, you can take multiple courses, but we recommend consulting with our advisors to manage your workload effectively.
